University of Melbourne Social Survey

A simple webpage that displays the location and images of the University of Melbourne Social Survey conducted by Wilfred Prest between 1941 - 1943. This site only includes survey data from Coburg and Pascoe Vale, Victoria.

Select the interview dates you want to display

Select a marker from the map to view the survey image
